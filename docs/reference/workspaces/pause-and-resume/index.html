Pause and resume

You can pause and resume your workspaces. Pausing a workspace keeps the storage around, but stops the running machine. You can then resume a workspace to start the machine back up.

Pause#

From workspace list#

  1. From the workspace list page, click the three dots to get the list of actions.
  2. Click pause. The workspace will switch to a pausing state and will stop at paused.

From workspace detail#

  1. From the workspace detail page, click the Onepanel icon

  1. Then click pause in the bottom pop-up

  2. The workspace will switch to a pausing, then paused state.

Resume#

From workspace list#

  1. From the workspace list page, click the three dots to get the list of actions.
  2. Click resume. The workspace will switch to a resuming state and then resume.

From workspace detail#

  1. From the workspace detail page, click the resume button

  1. The workspace will switch to a launching state and then finish resuming.
